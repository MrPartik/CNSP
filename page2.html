About us

The Child Neurology Society Philippines (CNSP), Inc. was formed in 1993 and is duly recognized by the Philippine Neurological Association, Philippine Pediatric Society and the Philippine Medical Association. It is likewise acknowledged by the International Child Neurology Association (ICNA) and the Asian Oceanian Child Neurology Association (AOCNA).

OUR MISSION
To champion the neurological health of all Filipino children

OUR VISION
Nationwide access to neurological expertise Empowerment of stakeholders in neurological health Optimal outcomes in children with neurological conditions

The Philippine League Against Epilepsy (PLAE) is a non-profit national organization of professionals committed to the improvement of the quality of life of persons with epilepsy through education, research, prevention, advocacy and delivery of optimal health care. The organization was founded in 1997, and in 1999 was accepted as the national chapter of the International League Against Epilepsy. PLAE is acknowledged as the expert source of information and continuing professional education in the care of persons with epilepsy in the Philippines.

OUR MISSION
We are committed to Pioneer Lead Advocate Epilepsy care.

OUR VISION
To be the CHAMPION of every Filipino with epilepsy.
